Vermeer's Delft

A visit to Delft 343 years after Vermeer

Vermeer was born and raised in Delft.

Homes
Born, childhood, adulthood

Vermeer was born and raised in Delft. There are no paintings here but two of his outdoor scenes can be found as well as his home and burial site.

Born

I took the Eurostar train here. You can take the route directly from London to Rotterdam. From Rotterdam, Delft is a 15 minute taxi or InnerCity train away.

Childhood

This small city is excellent. I spent a day and a half walking all over the city, exploring alleyways, taking a lot of photos and visiting the Vermeer-related sights. If I could, I'd move to Delft in a heartbeat!

I've made a more detailed page for this visit that covers all the specific locations, see the Vermeer's Delft page.
